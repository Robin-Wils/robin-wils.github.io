Todo list

There is a ton that I want to try. I am someone who likes to be busy. I am often busy with somethings, often with multiple things.

I can’t promise that I will achieve everything though.

I will try to only check my social media during Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays. I believe that social media is not good for you, besides that, it can eat a lot of time.

Friendly Premonition

Friendly Premonition is a fan project about Deadly Premonition. There is still a bit of work to do. I might live stream some coding at some point.

Read more about Friendly Premonition .

I will do this.

Deadly Premonition 2

I will play the brand new second Deadly Premonition game. I just have to. Deadly Premonition is just great.

I won’t put all my time in it, probably (since I want to do more things), but some of my time will get consumed by the game.

Other Godot project

I also have a own game idea based on the same code from the Deadly Premonition game, but that game idea will take so much time.

It won’t be for the next couple of years. I might work a bit on it, but I have enough other stuff to do.

Watch a bunch of movies

There are still a bunch of movies on my watchlist, and there will probably always be movies that I want to watch.

A tip for people who don’t know what to watch. Check who the directors of your favorite movies are and check which other movies they made.

It can also be useful to keep a list with your favorite movies (and directors).

You can find my watchlist at the bottom of the Movies, anime and series recommendations page.

I will do this.

Live stream digital art

I want to get better at digital art. I am currently very bad at it and live streaming could give me feedback and tips.

I will probably get much hate to, but that’s fine. I don’t really care about what most people think.

It is likely that I will do this.

Edit: I will probably not live stream it. I think that I found my problem with digital art. I will try to fix it.

I might share some stuff on pixelfed (see contact page). I didn’t really like how my previous ones looked. I am gonna go back to drawing on paper. I might color it in digitally.

Work a bit on this website

I will probably write some more articles or redesign some things, like usual.

It is likely that I will do this.

Buy a domain name

Codeberg is working on custom domain name support. I have some crypto from the brave browser. I do have enough crypto to buy a domain for a year.

I will wait until codeberg implemented custom domain name support and until I am confident enough that I would be able to pay for the domain name every year.

Read more about Brave on my Brave browser is actually pretty alright page.

It is likely that I will do this.

Make a short film

I want to learn film making. I am interested in practical effects and such, but I will start small.

I have an idea for a short film, but I am not sure if I will be able to realise it. I will probably start with trying a bunch of things first.

It will probably be a pretty bad film, but you have to start somewhere. It will be a ton of work, but I think that it would be fun too.

It is not likely that I will be able to do this.
