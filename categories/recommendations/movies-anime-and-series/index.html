Horror movies which are hard to watch

These movies have a great story, but it is also very disturbing. Most people probably want to avoid these. I enjoyed them though.

In fact these movies are pretty incredible is a way. These movies are not supposed to be fun. Martyrs is amazing, probably one of my favorites.

Horror movies to avoid at all costs

Read about these movies before you risk watching them!

Sure, these movies deserve to be seen by some people just like every movie. It are still movies, but you probably want to avoid these.

A Serbian Film, Cannibal Holocaust, August Underground trilogy, Visitor Q, Guinea Pig, Salò (or the 120 Days of Sodom), I Spit On Your Grave, Grotesque,…

That are the popular ones which you should avoid. Some people claim that you have to see Salò, since it is art and it won’t get out of your mind.

Honestly, read the wikipedia page about it first. I haven’t seen the movie. I might some day with people who can watch such a thing, but I will watch other movies first. It is not something which I want to watch immediately.

I would like to find local people who want to watch such movies though. I do really like the music from most of those movies. I think that some of those movies might have more story than it seems.

Look up the YouTube or Invidious channel “Nyx Fears” if you want to see someone ranking movies like these. I hope the video will convince you to not watch these. This stuff is not for everyone.
